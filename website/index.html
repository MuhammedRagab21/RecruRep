FAQ

Common questions

Who is RecruRep for?

Non-native English teachers looking for ESL teaching positions abroad. Whether you have 10 years of experience or you're just starting your teaching journey, RecruRep helps you find and apply to jobs faster.

How is this different from job boards?

Job boards show you listings but send you elsewhere to apply. RecruRep is end-to-end: browse, apply, track—all in one place. No re-entering your information, no bouncing between sites.

Is RecruRep free?

Yes. You can browse all jobs and apply for free. Premium plans with unlimited applications, AI-assisted cover letters, and priority support are coming soon.

What countries have jobs on RecruRep?

South Korea, Japan, China, Vietnam, Thailand, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Spain, Mexico, and more added weekly. We focus on countries with the highest demand for English teachers.

Do I need teaching experience?

Not for all jobs. Many schools hire new teachers for assistant roles and entry-level positions. You can filter by experience level to find the right opportunities for you.

Can I use RecruRep on my phone?

Absolutely. RecruRep is built mobile-first so you can browse jobs and apply from anywhere. No app download needed—it works in your browser.
