FAQ

Common questions

Who is this for?

Non-native English teachers looking for teaching jobs abroad. Whether you have years of experience or you're just starting out, this helps you find real openings faster.

Why only $1?

We want the first 15 people to actually use it, tell us what works, and help us make it better before the full launch. Your feedback shapes the final product. The $1 is just to make sure only serious people sign up.

How is this different from just searching on my own?

You can search on your own, but most teachers spend hours every week going through the same sites, finding the same dead ends. We do that work for you and put everything in one place. Think of it as having two teachers whose job is to find you jobs so you can focus on actually applying.

How do I know the jobs are real?

Every listing is checked before it goes up. If something looks off, it doesn't make it to the platform. No spam, no expired listings, no jobs that don't actually exist.

What if no new jobs get added?

We guarantee at least 50 new jobs every week. If we miss a week, you get your dollar back — automatically, no questions asked. You don't even have to ask.

Do I need teaching experience?

Not for all jobs. Many schools hire new teachers for assistant roles or entry-level positions. You can filter listings based on what fits you.

What countries are the jobs in?

Southeast Asia is the main focus — Vietnam, Thailand, China, South Korea, Japan — with more regions being added based on what teachers ask for.
