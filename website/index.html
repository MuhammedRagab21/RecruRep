FAQ

Questions teachers ask us

Is RecruRep really free to start?

Yes, absolutely. I believe every teacher deserves access to great opportunities regardless of their budget. The First Steps plan costs nothing and lets you browse all jobs and apply to five each month. Upgrade only when you are ready to apply more.

How does the one-click apply work?

You create your profile once—upload your resume, teaching certificates, and a cover letter template. When you find a school you love, just press apply. I bundle everything together and send it directly to the hiring team. That is it.

Do I need a work visa to use RecruRep?

Visa requirements differ by country, and I want to be transparent about that. Each job listing clearly states whether visa sponsorship is available. You can also filter jobs based on your citizenship and visa status when setting up your profile.

Which countries have teaching jobs on RecruRep?

I currently work with schools in South Korea, Japan, China, Vietnam, Thailand, the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Spain, and Mexico—with more countries added every month. I prioritize regions with strong demand for non-native English teachers.

Can I apply from my phone?

Of course. I built RecruRep to work beautifully on any device—phone, tablet, or laptop. Browse jobs, save opportunities, and apply right from your browser. No app download needed.

What makes RecruRep different from job boards?

Job boards send you elsewhere to apply. I keep everything in one place—browse, apply, track. Your profile travels with you, so you never re-enter information. I am not a listing site; I am your application companion.

I am a new teacher with no experience. Can I still use RecruRep?

Absolutely. Many schools hire assistant teachers and offer training programs for new educators. You can filter by experience level and find positions that welcome beginners. Every great teacher started somewhere.
