FAQ

Common questions

Who is this for?

Non-native English teachers looking for teaching positions abroad. Whether you have 10 years of experience or you're just starting, RecruRep helps you find real opportunities faster.

How is this different from LinkedIn or Indeed?

LinkedIn and Indeed are search engines — you go to them, you filter, you scroll. RecruRep is a curated feed. Jobs come to you, already filtered for eligibility, location, and type. No spam, no duplicates, no scanning page after page.

Why target non-native speakers specifically?

Non-native speakers face a different job market. Some roles are open to all, some are native-preferred — knowing the difference saves you from wasted applications. RecruRep's filter system is built around that distinction. Generic job boards don't offer this.

How do I know the jobs are real?

Every listing is reviewed before it goes into the database. If a listing looks suspicious, it doesn't make the platform. No spam. No dead links. No garbage.

Can't I just search for free on my own?

You can. But most teachers spend 1–2 hours per search session. Over a year that's 50+ hours of hunting. RecruRep does the work for $1 — curated, filtered, delivered to your dashboard while you prepare for interviews.

What if I don't find anything relevant?

If the platform hasn't been updated in 30 days, you receive a full refund. No questions asked. The risk is minimal either way.

Will I actually get hired from this?

We can't promise outcomes — that depends on your qualifications and the current market. What we can promise is access to more relevant opportunities than you'll find manually. More quality applications in less time means better odds.
