Privacy Policy

Information We Collect How We Use It Data Sharing Data Retention Your Rights Contact

Curric ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our platform, how we use it, and what rights you have over your data. By using Curric, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Profile information: Country, teaching experience, qualifications, preferred teaching locations, and any other details you add to your profile
  • Payment information: Processed securely via Stripe — we do not store card details on our servers
  • Communications: Any messages or feedback you send us directly

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and interaction patterns within the platform
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Log data: IP address, access times, and referring URLs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our job listing and application service
  • Match you with relevant teaching job listings based on your profile and preferences
  • Generate and deliver personalized cover letters for job applications you choose to submit
  • Process your subscription and send you account-related communications
  • Improve our platform, features, and user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or unauthorized use
  • Respond to your requests, questions, or feedback

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate our platform — including hosting (Vercel), database (Supabase), payment processing (Stripe), and email delivery. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Job applications: When you apply for a job through Curric, we share the information required for your application — such as your profile details and cover letter — with the hiring school or employer.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority, we may disclose your information.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove all associated personal data within a reasonable period, subject to any legal obligations or legitimate business needs (such as resolving disputes or enforcing agreements).

Payment transaction records are retained in accordance with applicable financial regulations.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights over your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data — you can do this at curric.app/delete-account.html
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities, such as direct marketing
  • Data portability: Request your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw that consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@curric.app. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include encryption in transit, access controls, and regular security reviews. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate our platform. These include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the platform to function — authentication, session management, and security
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform so we can improve it

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some platform features may not work properly without them.

8. Children's Privacy

Curric is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that we have collected data from a minor, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the country where our servers are based, your information may be transferred across borders. We ensure that such transfers are conducted in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by posting a notice on our platform and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of Curric after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
